Biography


James H Dean


Training:

Central School of Drama and Speech from 2009 to 2011.


Credits while training:

James played Oberon in the John Jack opera of A Midsummer Night’s Dream; The Pharaoh in The Royal Spa Centre’s production of Joseph and the Amazing Technicolor Dreamcoat; Bill in the Loft Theatre's production of Me and My Girl; Moonface Martin in Anything Goes at the Belgrade Theatre, Coventry and also regularly performed with the Leamington Voices.


Theatre credits:

James most recently reprised the role of Factor Girl touring the UK and Ireland in Cameron Mackintosh’s new production of Les Miserables, after having starred in the London original (Queen’s Theatre), returning to the production in which he made his West End debut; Deano in The Commitments (Palace Theatre) directed by Jamie Lloyd; King Arthur in Camelot (Bradford Grand); The Railway Cat in Cats (New London Theatre); George in La Cage aux Folles (Palladium); Tony in Billy Elliot the Musical (Victoria Palace Theatre); Raoul in The Phantom of the Opera (Her Majesty’s Theatre); Enjolras in Les Miserables (Queen’s Theatre); Collins in Michael Collins (Southport Opera House); Sweeney Todd (Gate Theatre, Southampton); Gaz in The Full Monty (Olympic Theatre); Tony in West Side Story (The Solitary Theatre); Declan in The Wireman (Gaiety Theatre); Don Lockwood in Singin’ in the Rain (Greenwich Theatre).
